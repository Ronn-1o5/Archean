ABOUT ARCHEAN

Archean is an Australian minerals company based in Perth, WA. Thanks to the strategic regional acquisition of abandoned gold mines throughout Western Australia, we have the unique ability to mine gold and tokenise the asset while it’s in the ground.

Heading up the team is Jacob Doutch, Melanie King and John Lester. Jacob Doutch, chair of the Classic Minerals Ltd Research and Development Committee, has over 10 years of experience in the mining sector managing large-scale projects. Melanie King brings with her a wealth of skills in education, case management, policy setting, human resources and community engagement. John Lester, who started his career as a stockbroker specialising in mining companies, has since gone on to become Managing Director at Golden West Resources Limited, spearheading the raise of more than $60 million in investments.
