WHO IS ARCHEAN?

Archean Resources is making it possible for investors like you to buy gold - below ground and below market price. We’re an Australian minerals company that has spent years exploring gold mines across Western Australia, targeting near-term gold projects and acquiring those mines with the most potential.

Since we created Archean, our aim has been to lower the barrier to entry of owning gold, to deliver significant value to every person that invests in the Archean Gold Token. By bridging the gap between the traditional gold industry and the innovative cryptocurrencies driving the modern world forward, we’ve made it possible for people from all walks of life to invest in gold.

COLLATERALISED CRYPTO

SECURITY FOR YOUR INVESTMENT

Unlike most cryptocurrencies, the Archean Gold Token is backed by a real asset - gold mined from the world’s leading regions. It’s a highly-tradable entity that provides an instant return, making it possible to invest in gold without going to a bullion dealer. Ultimately, collateralisation brings security to the famously volatile world of crypto.

JORC-COMPLIANT GOLD

YOUR QUALITY ASSURANCE

We’ve opened up the opportunity to own gold that meets the standards of the JORC Code. Investors have a legal right to every gram of gold they purchase, with access to information about the classification, reports, press releases, information memoranda and technical papers related to the resource.

3 STEPS TO INVEST

INVESTING IN ARCHEAN

With Archean, people from all walks of life can invest in gold. Whether you’re a seasoned crypto trader, investor of gold or just want to capitalise on this unique opportunity, the process is simple.

CREATE A WALLET

In order to buy and store any cryptocurrency, you’ll need to create a wallet. Having your own storage that’s separate from the wallets that crypto exchanges provide affords an extra layer of security and flexibility around how you hold your tokens.

VISIT COINSPOT

Visit CoinSpot, a secure Australian cryptocurrency exchange, or other third-party exchanges to compare and buy digital tokens. Here, you can buy the Archean Gold Token, where each token represents 1g of real-world gold. Create an account and deposit funds to get started.

MAKE AN INVESTMENT

Immediately start investing in gold-backed tokens. An asset that has stood the test of time, gold preserves wealth and offers more security compared to other, more volatile cryptocurrencies. Plus, with Archean, early investors can buy gold at a 30% discount before it’s extracted. Digital tokens are tradeable, swappable and investable with a single click.
